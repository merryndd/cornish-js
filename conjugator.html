This tool conjugates Cornish verbs, using the example regular verb dalla (‘to start’). Choose a person and verb form (tense/mood) and click Submit to see the corresponding conjugation of dalla. If you’re using as a basis for conjugating other verbs, note that mutation patterns should be adapted based on the examples.

The conjugations here are based on the verb paradigms in Gendall, Tavas a ragadazow (2000), adapted to SWF (LT) spelling.
